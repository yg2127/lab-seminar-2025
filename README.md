# Lab Seminar Presentations — Heart Lab (2025.06 – 2025.10)

Paper reviews and presentations I delivered during my undergraduate research internship at **Heart Lab, Sejong University** (Multimodal Affective Computing).

The papers were chosen to build foundations for our lab's work on **driver behavior understanding from skeleton sequences** (ST-GCN backbone), and to explore broader topics in **multimodal representation learning** and **self-supervised learning**.
